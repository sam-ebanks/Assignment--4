After graduation from Rhode Island School of Design, Lois opened a textile studio in New Jersey and became one of the leading textile stylist artists working in the New York industry. A vacation to the Cayman Islands changed her direction. She discovered a love of the tropics, relocated to Grand Cayman and began to paint the colors and light that fascinated her. Her textile business evolved blissfully into a life and love of painting. Lois often says she never imagined her life as a painter but now that she is an established fine artist, she can imagine nothing else.
